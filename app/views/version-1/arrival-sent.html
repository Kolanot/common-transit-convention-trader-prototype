What happens next

You need to:

  • email Border Force at the office where the goods have been presented (also known as the office of destination)
  • tell them that you are waiting for goods to be released
  • send copies of any paperwork to prove that duties and taxes have been paid or goods have moved to another customs regime
  • keep checking for the latest updates to arrival notifications to see when Border Force releases the goods

If an arrival notification gets rejected, you can view and fix any issues then send it again.

Contact Border Force at Teesport, telephone 01642 440 111, if the goods are not released or you have a problem.
